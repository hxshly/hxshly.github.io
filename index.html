Availability

How to login to Employee Self Service and change your availability

Login

Head to my.anfcorp.com and login with your Associate ID and Password.

Navigate to My Availability

After logging in, select 'Time & Attendance' from the main screen, then select 'My Availability' from the side bar.

Changing Availability

Select the week you want to change via the 'Availability for Week Ending' date picker. (This has to be at least 2 weeks away, as schedules are created 2 weeks in advance).

Current availability for the week you have selected is now shown in the top table, which can be modified below.

Select a day you want to change, set either Permanent (this week and all weeks following) or temporary (just this week), and then the type: either None, Any or Specify.

Once done, click save to submit the change request, or click on the plus button to add another day and follow the same method until that weeks availability is fully up-to-date.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • I can't login to my.anfcorp.com, but I'm sure I entered my username and password correctly?

    You might be unable to login for 1-2 days after your orientation, as your login details sync up with the website servers. If its been more than a few days and still not working, call the store and ask to speak to a manager.

  • I've done everything I should do, but it won't let me change my availability?

    The schedules are created 2 weeks in advance and availability changes are locked in on a Monday morning. Its likely that you're trying to change your availability for a day that falls within those two weeks, which is why it won't let you.

  • Can I park in the customer car parks?

    Meadowhall has free, dedicated staff parking a short walk from the M&S entrance, and the mall does ticket staff members who get caught parking in the customer carparks when working. If you download the Meadowhall Mallcomm app, you can register your car to gain entry to the free staff car park.

  • What do I do if my shift starts before store opening hours?

    If your shift starts before the store is open, call the store on 01142 568 485 and one of the managers will let you in.

  • I’m finding the online portal a bit confusing, what can I do?

    We suggest spending some quality time getting to know the online portal as soon as it will let you log on, if you do find it a bit difficult to navigate around, any manager would be happy to show you. The sooner you understand how to change your availability and how the schedule works the better as it will prevent unnecessary shift swaps.

  • I can’t work one of the shifts I’ve been given, what should I do?

    If you’ve been scheduled a shift that you are unable to work for whatever reason, we recommend trying to find cover or a swap on the Facebook group, you can request to join by searching for ‘Hollister Staff Sheffield Meadowhall’ or by clickin the link about.

Contact

If you need to get a shift swap or cover approved, or if you have any queries or questions, please call the store and ask to speak to one of the managers.
